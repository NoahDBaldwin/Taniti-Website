Lodging

Where to Stay

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government. To book a hotel click our "book your stay" link.

Hotels

We have a large variety of amazing hotels. From the luxurious Motel 6 to our not subpar Resort Taniti. Rates vary from $25 a night to $3,000 a night. Free continental breakfast is served daily.

Private Homes

Not as cool as a beach home, but we do have private homes that are a little cheaper. Get the privacy you need for your honeymoon and have the comfortablity as if you are right at home.

Beach Houses

Right on our south beach we have plenty of rentals and even bungalows on the ocean. Enjoy snorkeling only a short walk away from your door.

Bed and Breakfasts

Do you like to sleep? Do you like food? Well that's just what a bed and breakfast is. Come to some of our cozy locally owned bed and breakfasts to sleep, eat, and get on your merry way.

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.
